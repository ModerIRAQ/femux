# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Focus on ideas, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, or issues that violate this policy.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security/discussion channel with the maintainers and include relevant details for investigation.
